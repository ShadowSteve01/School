About me

I am a University student learning to make video games and trying to build up a small indie games company.
I have trained in game design in college at Westherts College and now have joined Bournemouth University to continue
my long journey into the industy. As many people can tell I am a huge fan of Doctor Who as it has inspired a lot
of my choices throughout the years, many of my influences have been part of the show such as David Tennat, Russel T. Davis
and Nicholas Briggs. In my spare time I like to create film media content and audio recordings that play tribute to
the Doctor Who franchise and I do this in hopes to be employed by them.
